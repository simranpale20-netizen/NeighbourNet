About NeighbourNet

A platform built on one belief — that every community has enough kindness to help its own people.

🤝
Our Mission

To connect people in need with willing helpers in their own locality — fast, free, and with zero middlemen.

💡
The Problem We Solve

People in urgent need don't know who to ask. Neighbours who want to help don't know who needs it. We bridge that gap.

🌱
Our Values

No money. No ads. No data selling. Just a simple board where humanity shows up for itself.
